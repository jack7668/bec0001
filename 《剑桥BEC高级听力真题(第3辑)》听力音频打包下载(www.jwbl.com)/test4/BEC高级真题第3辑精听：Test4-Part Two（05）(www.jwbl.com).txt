标题：BEC高级真题第3辑精听：Test4-Part Two（05）

听力内容：

Speaker Five 

第五位发言人

Woman: I used to work in a PR agency, doing publicity for lots of celebrities, and we were always too busy to pool our knowledge. Eventually, I instituted a working lunch once a week, to swap the latest information.

女子：我曾经在一家公关公司工作，我给很多名人做过宣传，当时我们总是在忙着搜集信息。最后，我就制定了每周一次工作午餐的计划来和同事交换最新信息。 

I'd realized that we needed something like that when I was publicizing one of our clients, and gave a journalist from a national newspaper the phone number of the person I thought was the client's booking agent. 

我意识到我们需要这样做是在给一位客户做宣传之后。当时，我把被我当做那位客户的经纪人的联系方式给了某全国性报刊的记者。

What I didn't know, but some of my colleagues did, was that the client had changed agents, so the wrong one then got hundreds of call. He was furious, so were our client and her new agent, and so was my boss.

我不知道，但有同事知道，的是那位客户已经换经纪人了。所以，后来那位被误当经纪人的人的电话都快被打爆了。他非常生气，我们的客户和他新找的经纪人也很生气，还有我的老板。 

All I wanted was to hide away somewhere until it all blew over. 

当时，我只想找个地方钻进去，等事情过去了再出来。
